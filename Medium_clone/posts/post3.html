5 Productivity Hacks That Helped Me Finish Projects Faster

By Aissata Galedou · April 2025

Finishing projects can be harder than starting them. Here are 5 tips that helped me stay on track:

  1. Set small daily goals: I focus on one small task a day, not the whole project.
  2. Work in short sprints: I use 25-minute Pomodoro sessions with breaks.
  3. Keep distractions out: Phone on silent.
  4. Celebrate small wins: I check off completed tasks and reward myself.
  5. Don’t aim for perfect: A working draft is better than a perfect idea stuck in your head.

These strategies helped me complete my portfolio site, side projects, and even write blog posts like this one.
